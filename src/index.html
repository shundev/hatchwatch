Crowdfunding for Families

Hatchwatch is a crowdfunding platform for families having kids.

Join ICO Get news letters

Basic concept

A Supporter selects kids, invest in them and receive tokens in return. The supporter can access the kids' private contents such as videos, photos and blog posts as long as he holds the tokens.

He can sell the tokens if the price goes up.

The families can widhdraw the capital and use it for their kids.
